VIP ROOM

Are designed to provide a superior level of comfort, often boasting king-sized beds with luxurious
linens, plush furniture for relaxation, and spacious bathrooms with high-end fixtures and amenities.

LOVE ROOM

Allows you to indulge in an enchanting place conducive to relaxation and letting go as well as
to romance and love...

DELUXE ROOM

A deluxe room is one of the most popular choices of guests staying in 5-star hotels
it offers comfort and amenities without being too over-the-top or expensive. It has at least two beds
which can fit up to four people so is perfect for those traveling in a group or for small families.

QUEEN ROOM

As the name suggests, queen rooms offer queen-sized beds that can accommodate two adults comfortably.
Some hotels sell queen rooms with two queen beds, so up to four guests can stay in one room.
By placing the bed in the center of the wall, the room allows for standard amenities such as a nightstand,
a desk or work area, a television, and a closet.

SINGLE ROOM

Guests are usually alone in the room and are using the space primarily for
the purposes of sleeping and getting ready to go out.

JUNIOUR SUITE ROOM

These suites include small living areas that extend off the bedroom space,
but they do not have dining areas.

PUB ROOM

Is a room with serve draught beer or cider without requiring food be consumed.
Have at least one indoor area not laid out for meals.

FAMILY

The family room is designed to be a place where family and guests gather for
group recreation like talking, reading, watching TV, and other family activities.
